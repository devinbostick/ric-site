**TL;DR**  
LLM drift persists due to probabilistic inference + batch-dependent numerics. Batch-invariance patches numerics; **RIC** enforces legality-first emission (FIELDCAST → CHORDLOCK → PAS_s → ΔPAS_zeta → TEMPOLOCK → AURA_OUT → Phase Memory), yielding bitwise-stable, replayable outputs.
